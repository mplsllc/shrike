About

Shrike is a free, open WHOIS history database. We collect and preserve historical WHOIS, DNS, IP, and ASN data — making it freely searchable and downloadable. An alternative to paid services like DomainTools and WhoisXML API.

All data is available via the REST API and as periodic bulk exports. Licensed under MPLS Principled Libre Software v1.0.
