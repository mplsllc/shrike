Privacy & Data Handling

What We Collect

Shrike stores publicly available WHOIS, DNS, IP, and ASN data from authoritative sources. We do not scrape private databases or circumvent privacy protections.

Most gTLD WHOIS data is already GDPR-redacted by registrars. When personal information is redacted at the source, we store the redacted version.

PII Handling

Some ccTLDs still expose registrant names, emails, and addresses. When we detect personal information in a WHOIS response, we flag it internally (contains_pii).

  • No PII search. You cannot search by personal email, phone, or address.
  • Bulk exports redact PII. Personal contact fields are stripped from data exports.
  • Individual domain lookups return data as-received from the authoritative WHOIS server.

Removal Requests

Any individual can request removal of their personal information from our historical snapshots. We comply within 30 days.

We remove: registrant names, contact details, personal email addresses.

We do not remove: registrar names, nameservers, IP assignments, DNS records, or organizational/corporate registration data.
